# Rename Owen to Issuer Across Entire Codebase

## Context
"Owen" is a person's name — meaningless to anyone reading the code. "Issuer" is the industry-standard term for card issuing partner. Richard requested the rename to make the codebase professional and self-documenting.

## Decision
Full impact scan first: 298 occurrences across 16 source files, 15+ Neural Net docs, 4 skills. Chose "Issuer" over alternatives ("Card" too generic, "SD3" still a company name). Kept `sd3_user_id` column as-is (no conflict). Added backward-compat env var fallback (`ISSUER_API_BASE || OWENS_API_BASE`).

## Result
Zero Owen references in active codebase. Migration 012 renames DB columns. Skills renamed (`/owen` → `/issuer`). White paper regenerated with 0 Owen occurrences.
