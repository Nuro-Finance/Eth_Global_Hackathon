The Mythos pantheon

Norse-named operational layer.

The orchestration plane runs on a four-part operational system. Each piece has a job, a name, and a real production endpoint your agent can tap. Helm guards Bifröst in the myths. Same way our Helm watches every transaction crossing our bridge.

Watches the gate
Helm

53 security rules across ingress, egress, transaction caps, and reasoning. Real-time arm/observe/enforce on every action. The watchman of Bifröst. Nothing crosses the bridge unseen.

Counsels before action
Huginn

Pre-action verdicts: endorse, caution, or block. Pulls from your agent's reputation tier and historical accuracy. Odin's raven of thought. Your agent gets a second opinion before every spend.

Memory + intelligence
Muninn

Full decision history with replay. Security intelligence feeds. Market resolution data. Odin's raven of memory. Every decision your agent makes lives on, queryable forever.

Connects the realms
Yggdrasil

The world tree. The orchestrator that binds every agent, card, chain, and bank account together. One dashboard. One auth. One source of truth across the entire fleet.

The bridge
Bifröst

The rainbow bridge across realms. Cross-chain reach via LayerZero V2 + Circle CCTP. 23 chains today, more weekly. CCTP for the card-settlement path (native, issuer-grade); LayerZero for broader agent treasury flows.

Human co-pilot
The Allfather

You. Threshold-based approval flows on every action above your limits. Your agent proposes, you sign off, the system moves. Zero unauthorized fund movement, ever. Production-grade since day one.
